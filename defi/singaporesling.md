# Singapore Sling

- A wallet (a decoy), & a wallet-within-a-wallet (stealth wallet)
- External / Decoy is used for DeFi, etc
    - Similar to [Tequila Sunrise](tequilasunrise.md)
    - But that wallet focuse on stable coins.
    - And this one is for hard crypto like ETH
- Internal / Stealth is used for staging/safety
- Internal uses a PIN-protected passphrase
- The passphrase used is a **short** meandering passphrase

- [Seed](singaporesling.uuen)           
- [Passphrase](singaporesling25.uuen)  

- Seed and Passphrase are encrypted with some  medium meandering passphrase
    - Obtain the medium meandering passphrase
    - Find the [Recipe](caving) and **short** meandering passphrase
    - Decrypt the [seed](singaporesling.uuen) to get the seed phrase
    - Decrypt the [passphrase](singaporesling25.uuen) to get the passphrase
    - Restore to a new wallet (the decoy)
    - Add a passphrase-protected PIN to get the inner wallet.
